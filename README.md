# Minesweeper
New vision for classical Minesweeper from Windows

# Description
You are a vassal of the king of power, You are a veteran who has gone through a lot of battles and wars. The king granted you a small plot of land for your merits. Your possessions consists of a small settlement, a tavern, a church and an abandoned mine.
After some time, as you arrived at your possessions, from the side of the neighboring castle, surrounded by a handful of battered knights, the messenger came. He said that a hostile state attacked his master’s castle. Your settlement the next frontier of defense, on the way to the royal castle. The messenger leaves the surviving knights and jumps further into the depths of the state in order to convey the message to the king, so that he can organize a retaliatory strike.
You decide to build a defense. Your goal is to hold out until reinforcements arrive. The task is not an easy one, because you don’t even have a castle. After a little thought, you decide to start with the construction of a stone wall. This will require a large amount of stone, which means it's time to restore the mine.
You have to build a group of miners and warriors, to explore the mine, and stone mining. Before the enemy attack you have about a month left, in which you must manage to rebuild (turn) your settlement into a formidable stronghold of defense.

# Gameplay
You are the manager. The mine will be responsible for the main earnings of gold and stones, and the gameplay is just the sapper's gameplay.
Each party member will have his own experience. For victories over monsters in the mines, everyone is awarded experience for diligence.
Monster if very strong is able to kill the war.
Then you are in the tavern of your settlement for a new fighter, who will be 1-2 levels.
In exchange for his pumped.
But in the future there will be an opportunity to resurrect the affected members of the squad. When the magic shop or a pumped church will be rebuilt.
For this, there is a cemetery.
In the future, in the tavern it will be possible to find mercenaries, doctors, demolitionists and magicians. Every day someone will be replaced by another.
You are gathering a group to make money, to spend money on improving the settlement and hiring a group.
Your party will be divided into two types: combat and non-combat. Not combat - these are miners, combat is all the rest.
